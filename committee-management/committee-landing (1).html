FAQ

Common Questions

How do committees work on this platform?
A committee is a group savings arrangement where members contribute a fixed monthly amount. Each month, one member receives the total collected (their "turn"). CommitteeHub manages tracking, payment verification, and turn scheduling.
How are payments verified?
Members upload a bank transfer screenshot. The admin or focal person reviews and marks it verified. This creates a full audit trail for every transaction.
How is turn selection decided?
Turns can be pre-assigned when a member joins, or decided through the spin wheel for fairness. All members can see the result in real time.
Is my payment information secure?
CommitteeHub uses CNIC-verified registration and secure data storage. Note: all financial transactions happen directly between members and admins. The platform records them but does not process payments.
What if a member doesn't pay on time?
Automated reminders are sent before the due date. If payment is missed, the member is flagged and the admin is notified. Penalty rules are defined by the committee admin.
How do I request to join a committee?
Click "Request to Join" on any open committee. Fill in your name, CNIC, phone, email, and address. The admin will approve or reject your request and you will be notified.
